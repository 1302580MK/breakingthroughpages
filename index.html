How far would you go?

I would like to test with you different approaches or elements which could be implemented in to an interactive story. Let's see how you like them. Please consider taking the survey after you tested a specific approach. The information gathered (anonymized) will be part of my thesis.

E-Mail

In example 1 you have to request a password by writing an email. The code will allow you to continue with the story.

In example 2 you request a keyfile which needs to be stored on your device and then uploaded by yourself to continue with the story.

-->

Example 1

Finally, Mr. Misterious has provided you with the crucial clue. Now all you have to do is download the evidence. Oh no, we need a password! Mr. Misterious didn't say anything about a password! Contact him again to get it. Hurry!

Write Mail to Mr. Misterious

You will recieve a Mail from Mr. Misterious.



Example 2

Finally, Mr. Misterious has provided you with the crucial clue. Now all you have to do is download the evidence. Oh no, the site is encrypted! Mr. Misterious didn't say anything about that. We need to upload a keyfile to unlock the content! Contact him again to get it. Hurry!
